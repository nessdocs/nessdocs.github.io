Introduction

Hi, my name is Vanessah Howard! I'm a technical writer that graduated from San Francisco State University in 2020. (B.A. in Technical & Professional Writing & Minor in Japanese).

Relevent classes I've taken:

  • [SFSU] CSC 210: Introduction to Programming in Java
  • [SFSU] TPW 480: Technical Documentation & Procedures
  • [SFSU] TPW 555: Document Design
  • [Udemy] Technical Writing: How to Write Using DITA XML (JPDocu School of Technical Writing)

I enjoy the challenge that technical writing brings and how creative I have to get to produce the best documents possible. It's not just the act of writing I love. I love figuring out how to organize information, structure documents, and meet the needs of the audience.

This portfolio website showcases my various writing skills, computer literacy, and overall technical abilities. You're actually looking at the first sample right now! I built this site from scratch using HTML and CSS. I've also made it available as a template on Github; there's a link to it in the nav bar.
